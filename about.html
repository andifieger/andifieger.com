I'm a Philadelphia born and raised girl living in Boston. After high school, I moved to Boulder, Colorado to attend University of Colorado with a focus in Media Arts. When I wasn't in class, you could usually find me skiing one of the phenomenal ski resorts located in my backyard.

After college, I landed an amazing opportunity to work with a creative interactive startup based out of Boulder, CO. After two and half years working on some amazing projects for clients like Disney Research and Design, Disney Mobile and Nickelodeon, I headed East.

I currently work for Arnold Wordwide as a Digital Producer on the Jack Daniel's team.
