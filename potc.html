Tombolo Interactive was commissioned to create a fully realized, 3D walkthrough of the Pirates of the Caribbean ride, scheduled to open in 2015 at the Disneyland Shanghai Resort. The application included such interactive features as Internet-based collaboration, so ride designers could interactively move through the simulation and makes notes, edit ride features in real time such as actual lighting, fog and animation effects done as they will appear in the actual ride. In addition, our team created the ability to test motion for ride features along multiple pathways and on multiple time waypoints.
