The Breakfast Brigade is a series of educational applications designed to stimulate through various activities including connect the dots, mazes and find the difference.

Drawing With Frenchie iPad app lets kids explore their creativity through drawing games and activities led by Frenchie, a piece of very fun french toast. Frenchie’s letter search, mazes, dot to dots, searches and find-what’s-different activities—all set to French music, of course—are designed to help hand-eye coordination and strengthen skills related to detail and focus. When kids are finished, Drawing with Frenchie’s camera button makes it easy to export and share artwork.
