It’s the Thought that Counts is a Jack Daniel’s campaign celebrating gifts that delightfully missed the mark over the holiday season. We challenged users to submit an image of their best, worst gift for a chance to win one of five custom sleds. The site saw over 300,000 visitors, 4,000 submissions and 18,000 votes.
